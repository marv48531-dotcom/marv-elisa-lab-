CANCER

CONTENT
  • The Causes Of Cancer
  • The Ways Of Treating Cancer

    • The biggest threat to the human life form is a cellular disease that begins when a cell or a group of cells(tissue) escapes the body's normal growth controls. When this happens, the cells grow into a tumor, a mass of growiing unregulated cells. Tumors are lump of tissues which can be cancerous or not(benign).
    Cancerous tumors spread into or invade nearby tissues and can travel distant places in the body to form new tumors(a process called metasis). Cancerous tumors may also be called malignant tumors. Many cancer form solid tumors, but blood cancer(leukemia) generally do not.
    Benign tumors don't spread into nearby tissues at all. However, benign tumors can grow extremely large, but once they has been removed, they can't grow back again.

    Normally, human cells grow and multiply (through a process called cell division) to form new cells as the body needs them, when cells grow old or become damaged, the die and new cells take place, which are backed up by a prtein, p53.
    Sometimes this orderly process breaks down and abnormal cells grow and multiply when they shouldn't.

    CAUSES OF CANCER


    Cancer can be caused by many different ways, which include:
  • Virus(A Biological Cause)
  • Carcinogens(A Chemical Cause)
  • Radiation(A Physical Cause)
  • Inactive P53 Protein(A Genetic Cause)
    • *Cancer is also a genetic disease because it can be caused by changes to genes that control the way the cell functions in our body, especially how they grow and divide

    WAYS OF TREATING CANCER


    Scientists and physicians are still trying to find the way for curing different kinds of cancer, but for now we can adher to the primary cure for cancer, which are:
  • Through Surgery
  • Through Radiotherapy/Radiology
  • Through Chemotherapy
